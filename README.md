## üí° What It Does

This dashboard helps organizers and stakeholders quickly understand the success of the Speaker Series events by tracking:

* **Attendance & Engagement:** How many people registered versus how many attended.
* **Audience Satisfaction:** Average scores and feedback sentiment.
* **Speaker Performance:** Comparison of ratings across different speakers and topics.
* **Historical Trends:** Visualization of performance over time.
